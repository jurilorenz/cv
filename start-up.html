🌐 Taking charge of the website launch, I ensured a user-friendly and engaging online platform that highlighted our expertise and sustainable values.

🔎 To boost our online presence, I implemented effective SEO strategies, which led to our website ranking at the top of Google search results. This increased our visibility and organic traffic significantly, attracting more potential customers to our courses.

🎉 Thanks to the collective efforts of the team, "Online School ProFruket" achieved remarkable success, selling over 1000 online courses and becoming a recognized authority in the field of edible bouquets with a strong commitment to environmental preservation.
